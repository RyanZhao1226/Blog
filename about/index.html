About Ryan Zhao

Hello there! My name is Ryan Zhao, and welcome to my personal website! I’m a computer science professional specializing in back-end development. My interests span a wide range of topics, including programming, artificial intelligence, and big data. When I’m not immersed in the digital world, I enjoy exploring other hobbies such as snowboarding, photography, travel, and music.

A Bit of Background

I graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science from Southwest University and have been working in the tech industry for the past 4 years. I’ve had the pleasure of collaborating with a diverse group of talented individuals at various companies, ranging from startups to well-established corporations.

Professional Experience

  • Back-End Developer at Microsoft
    • Designed, developed, and maintained scalable and high-performance back-end services for the company’s web applications, ensuring efficient data processing and storage.
  • Software Engineer at Microsoft
    • Collaborated with cross-functional teams to build and optimize the back-end infrastructure of the company’s core products, contributing to improved user experience and increased customer retention.
  • Senior Back-End Developer at Microsoft
    • Leading a team of developers in the design and implementation of robust, secure, and scalable back-end solutions for the company’s suite of software products.

Technical Skills

  • Programming Languages: Python, Java, Ruby, C++, JavaScript
  • Frameworks: Django, Flask, Ruby on Rails, Spring, Express
  • Databases: PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB, Redis
  • Other Tools: Docker, Kubernetes, AWS, Google Cloud Platform, Apache Kafka
